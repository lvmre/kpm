Mining and Vaulting

Mining and Vaulting

Kavai Precious Metals (KPM) Limited is proud to offer a comprehensive suite of services that combines precious metal Minting and Vaulting through a network of global strategic partners with trusted third-party providers. This integrated approach allows us to provide end-to-end solutions for clients looking to create and store their precious metal products.

We offer secure storage for precious metals and customizable minting services, including bullion coins and bars, to meet the needs of all our clients. Minting

Kavai Precious Metals (KPM) Limited offers professional and high-quality minting services for precious metals, including gold, silver, platinum, and palladium. Our minting services provide clients with the opportunity to create customised coins and bars, tailored to their specific needs and preferences.

Key aspects of our precious metals minting with global strategic partners include:

Customised Design and Production: Our global strategic partners collaborate with clients to develop and create customised designs for their precious metal products. From coins to bars, we ensure that the minted products meet the specific requirements and preferences of our clients. Our partners have the capabilities and expertise to produce high-quality minted products with precise weights, purity levels, and intricate artwork.

Advanced Minting Facilities: Our strategic partners operate modern and technologically advanced minting facilities. These facilities are equipped with state-of-the-art machinery and tools, allowing for efficient and precise production of precious metal products. The facilities adhere to the highest industry standards, ensuring exceptional quality and craftsmanship in the minting process.

Diverse Range of Precious Metals: Through our global strategic partnerships, we can mint a wide range of precious metals, including gold, silver, platinum, and palladium. This flexibility enables us to cater to the diverse needs of our clients, whether they require coins, bars, or other unique minted products in various sizes and denominations.

Rigorous Quality Control: Our partners follow rigorous quality control processes to ensure that each minted product meets the highest standards. This includes assaying and testing of the precious metals to verify their purity and authenticity. Clients can be confident that their minted products are of exceptional quality and meet the specifications they require.

Worldwide Distribution: With our global strategic partners, we offer worldwide distribution capabilities for minted precious metal products. This allows clients to reach global markets and expand their customer base, supported by efficient logistics and transportation services. By collaborating with global strategic partners, Kavai Precious Metals (KPM) Limited is able to provide clients with access to renowned and reliable minting facilities worldwide. Our partnerships enhance our ability to deliver customised and high-quality precious metal products, tailored to meet the unique requirements and preferences of our clients. Vaulting Secure Precious Metal Vaulting through our global strategic partners, we provide secure storage solutions for the precious metal products minted by our clients. Our partners operate secure vaulting facilities with state-of-the-art security systems and protocols to safeguard the stored assets. These vaults meet or exceed industry standards and offer 24/7 monitoring, access controls, and comprehensive insurance coverage.

Some key aspects of our precious metal vaulting partnerships include: Global Coverage: Our strategic partners are located in various strategic locations worldwide, providing our clients with the flexibility to choose the most suitable vaulting facility based on their specific needs. This ensures that their precious metals are stored in a secure location that is conveniently accessible to them or their desired markets.

Compliance with industry standards: Our partners meet or exceed industry standards for vaulting and storage facilities. They adhere to strict security protocols, including surveillance systems, access controls, and robust risk management practices, to ensure the highest level of security for the stored precious metals.

Insurance Coverage: Our global strategic partners provide comprehensive insurance coverage for the precious metals stored in their facilities. This offers an additional layer of protection for our clients' assets and provides peace of mind in the event of any unforeseen circumstances. Integrated Services: Through our partnerships, we offer integrated services that go beyond vaulting, including secure transportation, assay and testing, inventory management, and reporting. These services ensure efficient and seamless management of our clients' precious metals throughout their storage journey.

Efficient Communication and Reporting: We maintain strong communication channels with our global strategic partners to ensure transparency and seamless coordination in the delivery of our vaulting services. Regular reporting and updates on the status of the stored precious metals are provided to our clients, giving them clear visibility and control over their assets

Kavai Precious Metal (KPM) Limited is a registered and established mining company with operations in both Africa (Zimbabwe and South Africa) and Europe (United Kingdom).
