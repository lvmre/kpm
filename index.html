About us!

Read more

Kavai Precious Metal (KPM) Limited is a registered and established mining company with operations in both Africa (Zimbabwe and South Africa) and Europe (United Kingdom). KPM has been guided by strong operational ethics of integrity, transparency and a focus on positioning KPM as a strategic stakeholder in the value chain of base and rare metals within global markets.

The company’s objectives are to maximise shareholder value through exploration, discovery and development or commercialisation of opportunities in Base and Rare metals in Zimbabwe and beyond. At KPM, we understand the importance of staying at the forefront of industry practices and technological advancements. To this end, we continuously invest in the training and development of our personnel skills and knowledge necessary to leverage new opportunities and navigate evolving market dynamics

headquarters & Location

Kavai Precious Metals (KPM) Limited's headquarters is located in Harare, Zimbabwe. KPM has several satellite offices strategically positioned across Zimbabwe along the Great Dyke reef mineral-rich areas of the country. These offices enable KPM to have a local presence in the areas where we operate, improving the company’s ability to engage stakeholders, manage operations ,conduct efficient exploration, development, and mining activities. KPM has established an Investment Portfolio Management office in South Africa. This office serves as the hub of our investment activities while also supporting mining operations in Zimbabwe. The South African office allows KPM to tap into a wider network of investors and gain a comprehensive understanding of the dynamics of the global mining industry.

KPM has a global markets office for Commodities located in the United Kingdom. The office serves as the main hub for commodities trading activities, providing access to global markets and increasing the ability to serve the needs of clients worldwide. By strategically establishing offices in Zimbabwe, South Africa, and the United Kingdom, Kavai Precious Metals (KPM) Limited leverages local expertise, expands our network, and enhances our ability to deliver value to our stakeholders across the mining value chain.
