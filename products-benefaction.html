Products Beneficiation (Export & Value Added)

Kavai Precious Metals (KPM) Limited understands the importance of maximising the value of extracted and refined metals. In addition to our mining and processing operations, we also offer products beneficiation services to add further value to the metals we produce.

Our products beneficiation services encompass a range of processing options that are tailored to meet the specific needs of our clients. These services include:
Export Preparation: We ensure that the metals we produce are prepared to meet international export standards. This involves processes such as packaging, quality testing, and complying with relevant documentation and regulations to facilitate smooth exportation to global markets.
Value-Added Products: We recognize the potential for value enhancement by transforming the metals we extract into value-added products. This can include the production of jewellery, such as gold or silver ornaments, customised pieces, or other refined metal products that cater to specific market demands.
Custom Processing: We work closely with our clients to provide custom processing options, tailoring the beneficiation process to their unique requirements. This may involve refining metals to specific purities, transforming them into alloys, or applying specialised treatments to achieve desired characteristics.
At KPM, we leverage our expertise in processing and refining to offer a comprehensive range of product beneficiation services.Our products' beneficiation services are aimed at maximising the economic potential of the metals we extract, ensuring that they meet the highest quality standards and cater to diverse market demands.
By adding value through customised processing options, we enhance the market opportunities and profitability of our metals to the global economy.

Kavai Precious Metal (KPM) Limited is a registered and established mining company with operations in both Africa (Zimbabwe and South Africa) and Europe (United Kingdom).
