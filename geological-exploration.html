Geological Exploration

At Kavai Precious Metals (KPM) Limited, we offer a comprehensive range of geological exploration services that utilise state-of-the-art technology and tools. Our expert teams work closely with clients to provide mineral surveying, geological mapping, site characterization, and mineral resource evaluation. Mineral Surveying: We conduct comprehensive surveys to identify potential mineral deposits. By utilising advanced geospatial technology and remote sensing techniques, we efficiently map large areas and identify areas of interest for further exploration.

Geological Mapping: Our experienced team of geologists performs detailed geological mapping to understand the geological structure and characteristics of the target area. This enables us to identify areas with high mineralization potential and develop a thorough understanding of the mineral-bearing formations.

Site Characterization: Through site characterization, we assess the geological, geochemical, and geophysical properties of specific locations. This involves collecting samples, conducting geophysical surveys, and analysing data to understand the mineralization patterns in the area. Mineral Resource Evaluation: With expertise in resource estimation and modelling, we provide accurate evaluations of mineral resources. Our team utilises industry-standard techniques to estimate mineral quantities, grades, and qualities, allowing clients to make informed decisions about the economic viability of potential mining projects.

Kavai Precious Metal (KPM) Limited is a registered and established mining company with operations in both Africa (Zimbabwe and South Africa) and Europe (United Kingdom).
